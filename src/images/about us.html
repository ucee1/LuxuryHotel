Welcome

Welcome to the Official Website of Gateway Computer Training Institute. We are happy to offer you our expertise in ICT Training. Our tried and true system is based off over 15 years’ cumulative experience shared between our trainers. We pride ourselves in setting up our students and clients for success in this world of technology and are sure that the learners will complete our sessions more prepared than they have ever been. Gateway Computers provides comprehensive and cost-effective training for individuals looking to take the first step toward new careers or those looking to expand their computer proficiency and IT skills in their current professions. The company is both an operator and franchisor of training centers that offer a broad range of technical training, web and desktop application courses for all levels in multiple delivery methods and formats. With courses available online and in traditional classroom settings, Gateway Computers has a flexible educational solution that fits your learning style and your schedule.

About Us

Founded in 2002 and headquartered in Beirut Road, Kano, Nigeria, Gateway Computers was named The Third Most Effective Computer Training Centre in Kano State by CPN in the year 2005. In 2006, the company moved some of its operations to the nation's capital, establishing new branches at strategic locations around the Federal Capital Territory: First, in the bustling satellite town of Kubwa, subsequently in the ever-growing town of Bwari and most recently, in Gwarinpa, one of the largest estate in West Africa. Other branches have been established in two other locations, Kano and Ekiti states.

Our Vision

The company's vision is aimed at reducing the level of computer illiteracy in Nigeria, by enhancing the computer proficiency and IT Skills of organizations or individuals and to be one of the most reliable provider of IT Skills training in Nigeria.
